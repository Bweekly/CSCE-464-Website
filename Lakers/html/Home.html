About the Lakers


The Lakers are an NBA basketball team that was officially founded in 1947.The lakers play in the Pacific division of the Eastern conference. The Lakers are currrently the record holder for the most consecutive wins with 33 straight. They also are tied for the most championship wins with 17. They are based in Los Angeles and play in the Crypto.com Arena. The team is owned by Jeanie Buss and coached by Darvin Ham.
